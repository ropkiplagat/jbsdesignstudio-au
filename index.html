Common Questions

BIM Outsourcing, Explained

What does BIM outsourcing actually involve — and is it right for my practice?

+
BIM outsourcing means engaging an external team to handle your Revit modelling, documentation, and coordination work rather than doing it all in-house. JBS works as a seamless extension of your team — using your templates, your naming conventions, and your project management tools. It's right for any practice, builder, or developer that wants to reduce overhead, scale capacity, or access specialist BIM skills without a permanent hire.

How do you ensure model quality and NCC compliance on Australian projects?

+
Every model JBS produces is reviewed against Australian construction standards and the relevant NCC volume before delivery. Our team has direct experience with Australian residential and commercial building codes, and we flag compliance risks proactively. For DA and planning submissions, we align to the specific council's requirements and ensure all documentation meets the expected submission standard.

What's included in the free first BIM model offer?

+
Your free first model covers a single standard Revit modelling task — typically a residential unit, small commercial tenancy, or equivalent scope. It's produced to the same quality as all paid work: fully modelled, LOD-appropriate, and ready to use. No watermarks, no limitations. It's our way of proving the quality before you commit to an ongoing arrangement.

How do you handle clashes between architectural, structural, and MEP models?

+
JBS runs federated clash detection using Navisworks, cross-referencing architectural, structural, and services models to identify hard clashes, soft clashes, and workflow conflicts. We produce prioritised clash reports with clear visual references and recommended resolutions — not just raw clash counts. Reports are formatted for your site team or project meetings.

What's the typical turnaround time, and how do you handle urgent requests?

+
Standard Revit modelling and drafting tasks are delivered within 48 hours of receiving a complete brief and source files. For larger coordination packages we agree on a programme at brief stage and hit it. Urgent requests — pre-site-meeting clash reports, last-minute DA documentation — are handled as priority jobs.
